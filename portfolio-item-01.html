Harp Repair

Busted-off edge

This Dusty Strings harp came in with extensive damage to an edge.

The broken bit was still attached, but the wood had sprung far away from where it needed to be.

A little creative clamping and some hot hide glue will do the trick.

Repaired structurally, ready for touch up!

This was a fun repair. It was a challenge to get everything lined up and clamped quickly after applying the hot hide glue. Hot Hide Glue(HHG) doesn't stay open very long, but it's perfect for these kinds of repairs.
